Before you’re sprawled on your deathbed, there are some things you really have to say. They’re not complicated. They’re not poetry.
  
They’re just short sentences with big meaning.
  
I hope they get you talking.

**40
  
** **“I wonder.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things040.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things040-300x176.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="176" data-orig-height="176" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Give yourself time to think so the time you spend doing things will be better spent.

**39
  
** **“Today was good.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things039.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things039-300x198.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="198" data-orig-height="198" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>If you can say it once, you can say it again. And again. And again.<!--more-->

**38
  
** **“I believe in this.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things038.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things038-300x193.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="193" data-orig-height="193" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>A god, a plan, a company, a person, an idea—you have to put your faith in something.

**37
  
** **“I’m not finished.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things037.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things037-300x159.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="159" data-orig-height="159" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Only you get to decide when your life’s work is done.

**36
  
** **“Thank you for making this possible.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things036.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things036-300x199.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="199" data-orig-height="199" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Because nobody does anything alone. We’re driven and supported and thwarted by others at every turn.

**35
  
** **“That’s enough.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things035.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things035-300x182.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="182" data-orig-height="182" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Food. Drink. Episodes of Law & Order. Pairs of shoes. Overtime. Articulating your own limits is powerful.

**34
  
** **“I can do better.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things034.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things034-300x185.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="185" data-orig-height="185" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>As soon as you say it, you’re that much closer to making it true.

**33**
  
**“I’m sorry.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things031.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things031-300x239.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="239" data-orig-height="239" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>But you can’t just say it; you have to mean it. Really mean it.

**32
  
** **“I survived.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things033.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things033-300x181.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="181" data-orig-height="181" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Moments of danger are the plot points of an exciting life.

**31
  
** **“You’re amazing.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things032.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things032-300x189.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="189" data-orig-height="189" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Let yourself be in awe of another person, and you’ll feel strong and weak simultaneously.

**30
  
** **“I am home.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things030.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things030-300x178.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="178" data-orig-height="178" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Home is every adventure’s final destination and starting point—and we all need one to call our own.

**29
  
** **“I did my best.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things029.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things029-300x184.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="184" data-orig-height="184" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>If this is true, you did something amazing.

**28
  
** **“How can I help you?”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things028.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things028-300x170.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="170" data-orig-height="170" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Because you want people to come to your funeral, and if they can’t make it, at least they’ll miss you.

**27
  
** **“I’m lucky.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things027.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things027-300x168.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="168" data-orig-height="168" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>You are lucky, in a way that no one else is. Now, what are you going to do with your good fortune?

**26
  
** **“I want that.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things026.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things026-300x157.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="157" data-orig-height="157" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Ask for it: that’s you get what you covet—from others and for yourself.

**25
  
** **“This is wrong.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things025.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things025-300x202.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="202" data-orig-height="202" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>If you never say it, you embody the statement.

**24
  
** **“I quit.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things024.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things024-300x190.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="190" data-orig-height="190" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Not everything is worthwhile, and sometimes we don’t find that out until we’re in the middle of a rotten situation.

**23
  
** **“Isn’t this beautiful?”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things023.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things023-300x220.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="220" data-orig-height="220" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>The more often you notice the gorgeous world around you, the happier you’ll be.

**22
  
** **“Congratulations.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things0221.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things0221-300x190.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="190" data-orig-height="190" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Say this without jealously. Practice if you have to.

**21
  
** **“Damn, I look good.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things021.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things021-300x196.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="196" data-orig-height="196" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>You come from a long line of people who convinced others to sleep with them. Remember that.

**20
  
** **“I can master this.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things020.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things020-300x167.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="167" data-orig-height="167" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>The ability to learn is the foundation of every other talent.

**19
  
** **“Hold the mayo.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things019.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things019-300x188.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="188" data-orig-height="188" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Ask for the little things on a regular basis and you’ll find that it’s easier to make larger demands on occasion.

**18
  
** **“This is who I am.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things018.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things018-300x216.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="216" data-orig-height="216" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>The nervous energy spent pretending to be something you’re not is better spent on practically anything else.

**17
  
** **“Get out.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things017.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things017-300x171.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="171" data-orig-height="171" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>It’s always harder to take back an invitation than to give one, but protecting yourself from personified trouble is always worth the effort.

**16
  
** **“That was my contribution.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things016.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things016-300x151.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="151" data-orig-height="151" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Own what you’ve worked to create—that’s how your presence will be felt long after you’re gone.

**15
  
** **“I’ll try it.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things015.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things015-300x181.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="181" data-orig-height="181" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Consider the impotence of never saying you’ll try.

**14
  
** **“Tell me more.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things014.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things014-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="200" data-orig-height="200" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Really getting to know someone (or some topic) will help you better triangulate your own place in the world.

**13**
  
**“This is my favorite thing.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things013.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things013-300x204.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="204" data-orig-height="204" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Enjoy what you love and say this as often as you can.

**12
  
** **“I earned this.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things012.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things012-300x211.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="211" data-orig-height="211" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>There’s a layer of proud ownership over everything you possess that wasn’t merely given to you.

**11
  
** **“I don’t care.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things011.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things011-300x180.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="180" data-orig-height="180" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Being able to discern between what’s important and what’s trivial is a skill that will save your sanity and your schedule.

**10
  
** **“Your secret is safe with me.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things010.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things010-300x172.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="172" data-orig-height="172" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Because it feels deep-down good to be trustworthy.

**9
  
** **“Eureka!”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things007.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things007-300x176.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="176" data-orig-height="176" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Being the first to know something is a delicious sensation.

**8
  
** **“Let’s go!”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things009.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things009-300x208.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="208" data-orig-height="208" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Where you’re going often matters far less than the enthusiasm you have for the trip.

**7
  
** **“I trust you.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things008.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things008-300x160.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="160" data-orig-height="160" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>We all need allies, and admitting as much helps forge alliances.

**6
  
** **“I don’t know how to do this.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things006.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things006-300x211.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="211" data-orig-height="211" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>It’s better to admit it and learn than to fake it and embarrass yourself.

**5**
  
**“I’m terrified.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things005.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things005-300x169.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="169" data-orig-height="169" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Fear is an asset. It can save you from danger and alert you to trouble. Don’t ignore the tingles that run up and down your spine.

**4
  
** **“This is going to work.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things004.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things004-300x179.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="179" data-orig-height="179" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>When this is said truthfully, it’s an assertion of power.

**3
  
** **“I made a decision.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things003.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things003-300x186.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="186" data-orig-height="186" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>Autonomy transforms any activity from a chore to an act of destiny.

**2
  
** **“I love you.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things001.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things001-300x180.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="180" data-orig-height="180" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>We all want to say this, and we all want it said to us.

**1
  
** **“I understand.”
  
** <a rel="nofollow" href="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things002.jpg"><img title="" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jessicahagy/files/2012/10/40things002-300x167.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="167" data-orig-height="167" data-orig-width="300" /><br /> </a>More important than being right, or being important, is being truly aware.
